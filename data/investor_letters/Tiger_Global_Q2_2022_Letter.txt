To our Limited Partners,

Capital constrained by short-duration risk mandates was forced to liquidate exposure to high-quality, cash-generative businesses solely due to statistical factor correlation — not fundamental deterioration.

We view this dynamic as a structural edge that our fund's three-year lockup mechanism is uniquely positioned to exploit. Because our capital cannot be redeemed on short notice, we are under no obligation to respond to market-level variance that is entirely divorced from company-level economics.

In Q2, we increased our position in several core holdings during periods of peak systematic selling. We recognize that periods of systemic liquidity withdrawal often result in indiscriminate selling of high-quality, free-cash-flow positive enterprises by market participants constrained by daily VaR limits or monthly drawdown caps.

Because our capital lockups extend over a 3-to-5 year horizon, we possess the structural advantage to act as liquidity providers during these localized dislocations, accumulating core thesis assets at compelling IRRs.

We do not trade on 5-day noise. Our capital is locked, our thesis is multi-year, and our resolve remains unchanged.
